Top 3 Movies

  • 20th Century Girl

    • This Korean movie became a favorite of many people because of its nostalgic and emotional story about first love, friendship, and growing up. It shows the innocence and excitement of having a crush during the late 1990s, where relationships were built through letters, phone calls, and genuine effort instead of social media. Many viewers love it because it feels relatable, reminding them of their own teenage experiences, first heartbreaks, and unforgettable memories. The chemistry between the characters and the unexpected emotional turns made the movie memorable.

  • Green Bones

    • This Filipino movie became a favorite of many viewers because of its touching story about humanity, forgiveness, and seeing the good in people despite their past mistakes. It explores the relationship between a prison guard and an inmate who is judged by society because of his crime. Many people appreciate the film because it shows that every person has a story and that kindness can exist even in difficult situations. Its emotional storytelling, realistic characters, and meaningful message about compassion made it stand out.

  • Love Untangled

    • This Korean movie became a favorite because of its charming and heartwarming approach to young love, self-confidence, and accepting yourself. It captures the awkward but sweet moments of having a crush and trying to navigate feelings while dealing with insecurities. Many viewers enjoy it because it feels light, relatable, and nostalgic, bringing back the excitement of first love and the process of discovering your own worth. Its cute romance, lovable characters, and comforting atmosphere make it an easy movie to love.
